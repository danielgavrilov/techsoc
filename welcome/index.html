Thanks for joining TechSoc

Thank you for deciding to become a TechSoc member. As a part of your membership, you're welcome to attend any event we hold, from tech talks to office tours. Your support actively helps fund this society.

So, what's next? Firstly, you'll probably want to follow us on social media for the latest updates and photos (links at the bottom of this page). Contact us if you'd like to be on the mailing list if you're not already, so you can get information on the latest events right in your inbox.

Finally, please do join our Slack channel, where you'll be able to chat with other TechSoc members. It's pretty fantastic!
